Botox: Not Just Corrective, But Preventative Too

When most people think of Botox, they imagine it as a treatment to smooth out existing wrinkles and fine lines. While Botox certainly excels at this, there is a growing trend among younger clients who are turning to Botox as a preventative measure to maintain youthful, wrinkle-free skin.

At Beauteous Beauty Salon Kimberley, we offer professional Botox treatments starting from just R80 per unit. Our Botox services include 25-unit treatments at R2000 and 50-unit treatments at R4000. Whether you are looking to correct existing wrinkles or prevent new ones from forming, our experienced team can help you achieve natural-looking results.

How Does Preventative Botox Work?

Preventative Botox works by relaxing the muscles that cause repetitive facial movements, such as frowning, raising eyebrows, and squinting. By treating these muscles early, before deep wrinkles have formed, you can significantly slow down the ageing process and maintain smoother skin for longer.

The key benefits of preventative Botox include maintaining a youthful appearance, reducing the need for more intensive treatments later, and achieving subtle, natural-looking results that simply keep you looking refreshed and well-rested.

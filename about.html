Our Story

About Beauteous

Beauteous Beauty Salon Kimberley was founded with a simple vision: to create a space where everyone can feel beautiful, pampered, and confident. Located in the heart of Hillcrest, Kimberley, we have grown into one of the most trusted beauty salons in the Northern Cape.

Our team believes that "Content is King, but engagement is Queen and the lady runs the house!" This philosophy drives everything we do — from the quality of our treatments to the personal attention we give each client.

Whether you are looking for a relaxing facial, stunning lash extensions, flawless permanent makeup, or advanced Botox treatments, you are in expert hands at Beauteous Beauty Salon.
